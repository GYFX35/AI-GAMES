Game Overview

Welcome to the world of competitive ice dancing, brought to life through the magic of Augmented Reality. Our game lets you experience the grace and athleticism of ice dancing right in your own space. Create your own routines, compete against AI opponents, and climb the leaderboards to become a champion.

Core Features

  • Augmented Reality: Watch your customized ice dancers perform their routines on any flat surface.
  • AI Judging: Our advanced AI analyzes your routines and provides feedback on your performance.
  • Blockchain Integration: Own and trade unique costumes, music, and choreographies as NFTs.
  • Cross-Platform: Play on the web, mobile, and VR headsets.
  • Global Competitions: Compete against players from around the world in seasonal tournaments.

Technology Stack

This game will be built using a modern, web-first technology stack:

  • HTML5 & JavaScript: For a rich, interactive experience.
  • WebXR API: To power the augmented reality features.
  • Web3 Libraries: For seamless integration with blockchain networks.
  • Advanced AI Models: For the AI storyteller and quest generation.
