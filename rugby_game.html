Game Overview

Welcome to the future of sports gaming. Our AR Rugby game combines cutting-edge technology with the visceral excitement of the sport to deliver an unparalleled experience. Play in your world, build your team, and compete for glory.

Core Features

  • Augmented Reality: Transform any flat surface into a rugby pitch.
  • AI Opponents: Compete against intelligent AI teams that learn from your playstyle.
  • Blockchain Integration: Own, trade, and manage your players as unique digital assets on the blockchain.
  • Cross-Platform: Play on Meta Horizon, as a PWA on your desktop, or on your mobile device.
  • Career Mode: Take your team from the local leagues to the world championship.

Technology Stack

This game will be built using a modern, web-first technology stack:

  • HTML5 & JavaScript: For a rich, interactive experience.
  • WebXR API: To power the augmented reality features.
  • Web3 Libraries: For seamless integration with blockchain networks.
  • Advanced AI Models: For realistic opponent behavior and game simulation.
