Game Overview

Welcome to a journey through the vibrant and diverse cultures of Africa. Our AR game brings history, art, and tradition to life, allowing you to explore and interact with cultural artifacts in your own environment.

Core Features

  • Augmented Reality: View and interact with 3D models of African artifacts.
  • AI Storyteller: Learn about the history and significance of each artifact from our AI guide.
  • Blockchain Integration: Collect and own unique digital versions of the artifacts.
  • Cross-Platform: Experience the game on the web, mobile, and VR headsets.
  • Cultural Quests: Embark on quests to discover hidden artifacts and learn more about African cultures.

Technology Stack

This game will be built using a modern, web-first technology stack:

  • HTML5 & JavaScript: For a rich, interactive experience.
  • WebXR API: To power the augmented reality features.
  • Web3 Libraries: For seamless integration with blockchain networks.
  • Advanced AI Models: For the AI storyteller and quest generation.
